Overview

Real Estate Management Solution is a comprehensive solution for the real estate industry. The entire business process of either a real estate developer or a broker is managed and maintained from start to end by using this solution. It automatically integrates data about billing, payments, collections, vacancies, and transfers.

Real Estate Management Solution involves processes, systems and the manpower required to manage the life cycle of all acquired property, including acquisition, control, accountability, maintenance, utilization and disposition. It enables local property managers and maintenance personnel to manage day-to-day operations related to their properties.
