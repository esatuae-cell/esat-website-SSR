Contract Management Solution is used to easily handle the contract details of all types.

Overview

Contract Management Solution is used to solicit and analyze competitive bids. It’s an easy-to-use tool to handle the details of all types of contracts. The Change Order Management feature can track all adjustments to contract scope and control expenditures. It also provides an option of progress payments, which automatically calculates retain age, and real-time integration to Project Management and Financial Management features, which helps ensure compliance with quality and safety standards.

Features and benefits

Create agreements

Create and manage subcontractor agreements, including Statement of Works (SoW) for work status

History of agreements

Easily track the records of all agreements and compare them with other related agreements

Estimating Subcontractor

Retain subcontract data for future reference and allow price comparison

Automated reports

Managers can easily generate up-to-date reports, agreements, and all other documents relating to contracts

Reports, dashboards, and business intelligence.

The Contract Module's Group Reports and Dashboards assist businesses in making data-driven decisions by gathering, analyzing, and presenting data.
