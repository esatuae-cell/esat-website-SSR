With world-class Asset Management Solution from ESAT, you can efficiently manage and control the entire life cycle of your company’s assets.

Overview

Asset Management Solution handles all fixed assets-related transactions that are part of an organization’s Financial Accounting. This solution manages and controls the life cycle of company’s assets and the company’s performance, including the acquisition and classification of assets acquired. Moreover, Asset Management Solution performs quick calculations of depreciation based on any commonly used methods— in addition to asset revaluation and impairment, sale and disposal. The Asset Management Solution provides comprehensive recording, tracking and management. In other words, you will get real-time and accurate data on all of your company’s assets.
