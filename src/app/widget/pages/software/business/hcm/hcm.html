ESAT Human Capital Management Solution (HCM) offers a complete, compliant and modern solution to manage workforce more efficiently.

Overview

Human Capital Management (HCM) Solution handles the workforce management processes and employee data. It helps you give to the suitable employees, an access, to the right information, at the right time, and via the right processes. Integrated, scalable HR applications and processes can promote a consistent culture throughout your company, and facilitate planning and strategizing processes without compromising on local nuances and overall effectiveness.
HCM Solution can help your organization engage employees directly and hold them accountable for their actions and this may have a direct impact on the organization’s success. By the means of our HCM Solution, you can automatically engage your employees in various tasks without overburdening HR staff which can, meanwhile, work on other non-automated tasks.
HCM Solution helps you expand the value of your Human Capital Management (HCM) investment by combining HCM applications with tools created for Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems. Users can leverage information from HR systems and content sources such as policies, benefits summaries, employee competency and demographic data. By giving users access to these information, you will support best practices and other processes in your company, which will translate into more efficient management of the workforce, while helping your HR department focus on other tasks and be more productive using the time that our HCM solution—can save.
