Inventory Management Solution is a tool designed to manage, control and keep accurate records of inventory items of an organization.

Overview

Inventory Management Solution provides a real-time picture of inventory activities of the organization and enables the user to classify inventory items by group and category. This solution helps you improve and efﬁciently execute warehouse system. It will help you manage and control the quality of your inventory items, and reduce defects from the processes, thus reducing your costs—and every savings will be directly added to your proﬁt.

Inventory Management Solution also provides accurate physical counts of inventory items and allows you to track in-transit inventories. In this way, you will know exactly what inventory you have and where it is stored. You will be able to conveniently retrieve the inventory items promptly when ﬁlling customer orders.

Easy allocation & control and Bin Management Profile

Easy tracking and efficient inventory management

Provides helpful features to effectively track inventory items and activities such as the in- and out-of-inventory goods, along with the stock transferred, allocated, monitored & reservation, and release.

Eliminate rework, defects and wastage

This system supports you in implementing quality controls on inventory items using an inspection plan. This option will help your company eliminate and/or significantly reduce rework, defects, and wastage

Maximize storage area and minimize storage costs.

The effective tracking of inventory items and activities helps maximize storage area while minimizing storage cost by managing batch and expiry date.
