ESAT Manufacturing Management System recognizes the manufacturers’ need to operate seamlessly across multiple manufacturing modes.

Overview

ESAT has long offered manufacturers an agile solution for Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP). Right from the outset, ESAT recognized the manufacturers’ need to operate seamlessly across multiple manufacturing modes. Manufacturing has become more complex, and today, ESAT-ERP helps more and more manufacturers operate in any product combination such as: make-to-stock, make-to-order, conﬁgure-to-order, engineer-to-order, batch process and engineer-procure-construct.
