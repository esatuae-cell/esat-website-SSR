ESAT Organization Developement system provide a uniform and consistent method to be followed by employees working in the Organisation.

Overview

By this module, user can request Organization Development to Implement and Modification of Standard Operating Procedure, Training, Strategy/Planning/Review, Management Documents, Assessment and Audit, Survey, etc. Organization Development Module is used to easily handle the organizational flow of the company.
