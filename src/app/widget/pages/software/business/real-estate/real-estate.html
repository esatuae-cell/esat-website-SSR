This solution maintains and manages the entire business process of a real estate developer or a broker from end to end.

Overview

Real Estate Management Solution is a comprehensive solution for the real estate industry. The entire business process of either a real estate developer or a broker is managed and maintained from start to end by using this solution. It automatically integrates data about billing, payments, collections, vacancies, and transfers. Real Estate Management Solution involves processes, systems and the manpower required to manage the life cycle of all acquired property, including acquisition, control, accountability, maintenance, utilization and disposition. It enables local property managers and maintenance personnel to manage day-to-day operations related to their properties.

Features and benefits

Perform routine tasks easily

This solution is designed to help you easily perform routine tasks, such as collecting rent payments and handling maintenance requests.

Complete financial reports

You can have the complete key financial reports of your business along with income and expenses.

Real-time reports

Generate real-time reports whenever you need and maintain records.

An easy way to respond to clients’ requests

Customer Support is available to help you out along the way and make sure that you are able to respond to your clients’ requests.

Reports, dashboards, and business intelligence.

The Real Estate Module's Group Reports and Dashboards assist businesses in making data-driven decisions by gathering, analyzing, and presenting data.
