ESAT Workflow Designer and Engine System

ESAT Workflow Designer and Engine System is a full-fledged workflow management tool that allows administrators to implement complex workflows involving multi-level approvals, rule-based branching, etc., to all ESAT functions. This extends the customization and expansion capabilities of ESAT-ERP without affecting the main software.

Offers an extensive workflow system used to trigger notification perform system action & manage content approve in ESAT

Key features

  •   Set-up and manage complex workflow rules and logistics. This feature will allow you to easily handle complex and diverse business processes for every function of your organization
  •   Routing of workflows based on user actions such as approvals, rejections and update of core information
  •   Easily conﬁgurable approval requests email messages
  •   Reminders on pending approvals
  •   Inside/outside network approvals: enabling remote approvals on implementations within network
  •   Supports web service calls, WCF calls, stored procedure calls, SQL query builder, etc.
  •   Detailed tracking of all operations and maintaining audit log/history

ESAT Energy Dashboard System

ESAT Energy Dashboard System provides a highly detailed picture of energy utilization, water consumption, green power generations and monetary savings and leverages drill down process to provide detailed analysis.

Intuitive Management Dashboards are developed using Microsoft Silverlight technology which provides excellent user-experience and are a must-have tool for each individual user group & the top management and managers of green buildings and large facilities.

Key features

  •   Resource-wise consumption trends
  •   Alerts on consumption beyond set limits
  •   Bar/trend/pie charts for easy analysis
  •   View detailed consumption date with comparison
  •   Records history of increase or decrease in consumption, with relevant reasons which are entered into a knowledge base for future analysis
  •   Greenhouse gas/carbon accounting
  •   Reports on green building certiﬁcation
  •   Monetary savings reports
  •   Energy utilization threshold conﬁguration alerts in case of excess utilization
  •   Easy integration with popular BMS/BAS systems
  •   Custom integration with meters/devices that support popular protocols

Role-based workspace

  •   Personal home page
  •   Personalized by user or administrator
  •   Multi-level access to relevant data

Drawing Management

ESAT Drawing and Document Management System is a digital ﬁles management tool designed to store, track and manage information of all the drawings, documents, manuals, software, etc., associated with the facility/building system and equipment.

The floor plans, equipment drawings, building plans, technical drawings, wiring diagrams, network diagrams, etc., can be securely stored, maintained and managed through this system. The extensive search option helps identify and retrieve information or documents quickly.

The supported ﬁle types include AUTOCAD, DWG, BMP, JPG, PDF, DOC and many more.

Dynamic Reporting

ESAT-ERP comes with integrated solutions that enable organizations to provide dynamic reporting, providing end users with exactly what they want to see, when they want to see it.

Once you create a report, you can save it and it will automatically appear on your business analyzer dashboard, giving you an instant and updated view of your information. With a click of a button, you can access the up-to-date details of such report without having to ask for additional information.

Another beneﬁt of the advanced reporting options is the ability to share information effortlessly with other members of your team.
