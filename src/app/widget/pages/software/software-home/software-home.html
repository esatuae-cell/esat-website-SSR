What is an ERP System?

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) is business process management software that enables any organizations environment to improve their performance by using a system of integrated applications. ERP aims to unify the various functions into one complete system which then enables processes and information to be streamlined across the entire organization.

The business software tools are designed to simplify the flow of information and data between all departments within an organization, including departments such as those of Finance, Distribution, Accounting, Inventory Management, Sales, Customer Relationship, Marketing, Planning, Human Resources, Manufacturing, and other operating units.

ERP software has the ability to process information coming from different parts of the organization, and any type of transaction, within a single, integrated solution which can track in real-time business operations and provide timely and accurate information.

ERP software will enable your business to monitor its operations in real-time and provide you with the information you require anytime you need it. These factors make the ERP software an essential business tool in the competitive world we live in.

Advantages / Needs of ERP Solutions

The biggest advantage of ERP software is its real-time capabilities and ability it offers that enables you to see what is going on in your entire company.
