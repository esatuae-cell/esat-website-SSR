Fixed Assets Management -Training Course

This course delves deep into asset management systems and their vital role in maximizing the lifespan of physical assets in organizations. Since assets are critical in delivering value to businesses and industries, it is crucial to manage, maintain, and utilize them efficiently for operational efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and strategic decision-making.

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Audience: Asset Managers, Facility Managers, Operations Managers, Maintenance Engineers and Technicians, Supply Chain and Procurement Professionals
  • Prerequisites:Basic Computer Literacy, Familiarity with Business Operations, Understanding of Asset Concepts, Basic Knowledge of Data Management, Awareness of Industry Context
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • Asset Account Settings, Asset Master Information, Asset Capitalization, Asset Depreciation, Asset Depreciation Schedule, Asset Disposal Request, Asset Disposal, Asset Transfer, Gate Pass, Asset Barcode Print, Asset Insurance, Asset Revision, Asset Issue, Asset Handover, Asset Status Change, Asset Excess, Asset Excess & Shortage, Asset Rates Entry, Asset Import Export
