Budget & Project Management -Training Course

The Budget and Project Management Fundamentals course offers participants a comprehensive foundation in the principles and practices of effectively managing project budgets, resources, and timelines. This course equips learners with the knowledge and skills required to plan, execute, monitor, and control projects while staying within budgetary constraints and delivering successful outcomes.

Course Details

  • Duration: 2 Days
  • Audience: Project Managers, Team Leaders, Project Coordinators, Business Analysts, Department Managers
  • Prerequisites: Basic Understanding of Project Management Concepts, Familiarity with Business Processes, Proficiency in Communication Skills, Knowledge of Financial Principles, Awareness of Time Management Techniques
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • Projects, Cost Codes, Cost Code (Budjetted), BOQ, Estimation, WBS, Work Load, WBS %, WBS Master, Project Plan, Stream, Work Order Progress
