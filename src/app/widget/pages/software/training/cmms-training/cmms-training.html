CMMS -Training Course

The Computerized Maintenance Management System (CMMS) Essentials course provides participants with a comprehensive understanding of how to effectively utilize CMMS software to streamline maintenance operations, optimize asset management, and enhance overall facility efficiency. This course equips learners with the knowledge and skills necessary to implement and manage CMMS effectively.

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Audience: Maintenance Managers, Facility Managers, Operations Managers, Maintenance Technicians, Asset Managers
  • Prerequisites: Basic Understanding of Maintenance Management Concepts, Familiarity with Asset Management Principles, Proficiency in Computer Skills, Knowledge of Inventory Management, Awareness of Data Security Principles
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • Equipment Master Information, Sites, Check List, Packages, Team Setup, CMMS Inquiry, CMMS Quotation, CMMS Contract, Work Order Request, Preventive Maintenance Schedule, Inspection, Condition Based Maintanance ( CBM ), Work Order, CMMS Invoice, Equipment Import Export
