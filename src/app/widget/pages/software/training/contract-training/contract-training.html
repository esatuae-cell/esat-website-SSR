Contract Management -Training Course

The Contract Management Strategies course provides participants with a comprehensive understanding of the principles, processes, and best practices involved in effectively managing sub-contracts within the context of larger projects or business operations. This course equips learners with the knowledge and skills necessary to establish successful relationships with sub-contractors, ensure contractual compliance, and optimize project outcomes.

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Audience: Project Managers, Procurement Professionals, Contract Administrators, Construction Managers, Supply Chain Managers
  • Prerequisites: Basic Understanding of Project Management, Familiarity with Procurement Processes, Knowledge of Contract Law, Proficiency in Communication Skills, Awareness of Vendor Management
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • Business Partner, New Service Request, Subcontract Services, Service Purchase Request, Service Agreement, Service Inspection, Deduction Details, Payment Certificate
