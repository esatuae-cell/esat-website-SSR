Facility Administration -Training Course

The Facility Administration and Management course provides delegates with a comprehensive understanding of the principles, practices, and strategies involved in effectively managing and administering various types of facilities. This course is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to oversee the day-to-day operations, maintenance, and optimization of facilities to ensure their smooth functioning and to create a conducive environment for occupants.

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Audience: Aspiring Facility Managers, Current Facility Administrators, Facility Adminsitration Professionals, Building Owners and Operators, Facility Management Team Members
  • Prerequisites: Basic Understanding of Management Principles, Familiarity with Building Infrastructure, Knowledge of Occupational Health and Safety, Experience in Maintenance or Operations Roles
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • Facility Inquiry, Facility Quotation, Facility Contract, Facility Amendment, Client Final Settlement, Incident & Accident, Penalty Operation, Penalty Invoice, Id Card Settings, Operation Charges

Space Management -Training Course

The Space Management and Optimization course provides participants with a comprehensive understanding of the principles, strategies, and practical techniques involved in effectively managing and optimizing space within various types of facilities. This course is designed to equip participants with the knowledge and skills required to maximize space utilization, improve functionality, and create efficient and adaptable environments.

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Audience: Facilities Managers and Administrators, Interior Design Professionals, Office Managers and Administrators, Real Estate Developers and Planners, Business Owners and Entrepreneurs
  • Prerequisites: Basic Understanding of Facility Management, Familiarity with Design Principles, Background in Architecture or Interior Design, Knowledge of Workplace Ergonomics, Experience in Facility Planning or Layout Design
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • Property Owner, Accommodation, Acoomodation Category, Bed Status, Bulk Bed Status Settings, House Keeping Items, Housekeeping Discrepancy

Resident Management -Training Course

The Resident Management and Community Engagement course offers a comprehensive exploration of the principles, strategies, and practical skills required to effectively manage residential communities and engage with residents. Participants will gain a deep understanding of the responsibilities involved in creating a harmonious living environment and fostering positive relationships among residents.

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Audience: Property Managers and Administrators, Community Association Managers, Resident Managers and Building Supervisors, Real Estate Professionals, Housing Coordinators and Social Workers
  • Prerequisites: Basic Understanding of Property Management Principles, Familiarity with Tenant-Landlord Relationships, Background in Customer Service or Communication, Knowledge of Housing Regulations and Fair Housing Practices, Experience in Facilities or Community Management
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • Resident Master, Resident Check-In, Bed Reservation, Bulk Bed Booking, Bulk Dining Hall, Bulk Gate Assignment, Swipe Card Assignment, Swipe Card (Activation/De-Activation), Bulk Sign In / Check Out, Resident Check-Out, Accommodation Transfer, Contract Transfer, Other Transfer, Resident Status Settings, Bulk Temporary Exit Return Sign-In, Bulk Issues And Control, Stock Adjustment, Surgical Resident

Facility Services -Training Course

The Facility Services Management course provides a comprehensive overview of the principles, strategies, and practical skills necessary for effectively managing and optimizing facility services within diverse environments. Participants will gain an in-depth understanding of the various services that contribute to the smooth operation and overall functionality of facilities, while also learning how to enhance occupant satisfaction and streamline service delivery.

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Audience: Facility Managers and Administrators, Service Procurement Professionals, Operations and Maintenance Managers, Service Providers and Contractors
  • Prerequisites: Basic Understanding of Facility Management Concepts, Familiarity with Service Procurement and Vendor Management, Background in Operations or Property Management, Knowledge of Maintenance and Service Delivery Processes, Experience in Budgeting and Resource Allocation
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • Facility Group Master, Packages, Gate Management, Laundry Items, Catering Items, Dining Hall Setup, Packed Meal, Ramadan Setup
