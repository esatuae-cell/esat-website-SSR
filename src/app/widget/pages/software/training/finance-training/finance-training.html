General Accounts -Training Course

The Finance General Accounts course provides participants with a comprehensive understanding of the fundamental principles, concepts, and practices related to financial accounting and reporting within the context of corporate finance. This course is designed to equip participants with the necessary skills to interpret, analyze, and prepare financial statements, enabling them to make informed financial decisions and communicate effectively with stakeholders.

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Audience: Undergraduate Business participants, Finance Professionals seeking Continuing Education, Individuals interested in Understanding Financial Statements, Entrepreneurs and Small Business Owners, Investors and Stock Market Enthusiasts
  • Prerequisites: Basic Understanding of Accounting Concepts, Familiarity with Financial Terminology, High School Level Mathematics Skills, Proficiency in Using Spreadsheet Software, Introductory Knowledge of Business and Economics
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • Chart Of Accounts Request, Chart Of Accounts, Journal Voucher, Debit Note, Credit Note, Adjustment Against Advance, Prepayment Contract, G/L Budget, General Opening Balance, Personal Account Opening Balance, Associate Company Opening Balance, Other Opening Balance, Monthly Closing, Year End Closing JV, Audit Findiing, Cost Center Opening Balance, Cost Center Department Linking, Cost Center, Location Master, Account Categories, Account Types, Transaction Cut Off Type

Accounts Receivables -Training Course

The Finance Accounts Receivables course offers participants a comprehensive exploration of the critical role that accounts receivable management plays in a company's financial health and cash flow management. Through this course, students will delve into the principles, practices, and strategies necessary for effective management of accounts receivables, including credit assessment, billing, collection, and risk mitigation.

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Audience: Finance and Accounting Users, Financial Managers and Analysts, Credit and Collections Professionals, Small Business Owners and Entrepreneurs, Accounting and Finance Professionals Seeking Advanced Training
  • Prerequisites: Introductory Accounting Knowledge, Understanding of Financial Statements, Basic Finance Concepts, Proficiency in Spreadsheet Software, Familiarity with Business Mathematics
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • Business Partners, Business Partners Group, Customer Opening Balance, Receipt Voucher, Credit Note, Cheque Collection Status, Receivable Reconcilation, Client Database Evaluation
