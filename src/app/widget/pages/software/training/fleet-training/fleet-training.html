Fleet Management -Training Course

The "Fleet Management " course provides a brief yet comprehensive overview of principles and tools for effectively overseeing and optimizing a fleet of vehicles. Participants learn to utilize specialized software solutions for tasks such as route planning, vehicle tracking, and maintenance scheduling, enhancing their ability to manage and enhance the efficiency of fleet operations.

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Audience: Fleet Managers, Transportation and Logistics Professionals, Vehicle Maintenance Specialists, Operations Managers, Logistics and Supply Chain Staffs
  • Prerequisites: Basic Understanding of Transportation Operations, Familiarity with Fleet Logistics, Knowledge of Vehicle Maintenance Practices, Basic Computer Literacy, Foundation in Business Operations
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • Business Partners, Service Master, Vehicle Type, Vehicle Master, Fleet Inquiry, Fleet Quotation, Fleet Contract, Request/Complaints, Work Order, Preventive Maintanance, Driver Assignment, Equipment Issue To Fleet, Fleet Issue, Fleet Transfer, Fleet Hand Over, Transport Job, Trip Log, Fleet Invoice, Insurance Policy, Expense Entry Form, Fuel Entry Form, Incident and Accident, Fines and Penalties, Penality Invoice, GPS Tracking
