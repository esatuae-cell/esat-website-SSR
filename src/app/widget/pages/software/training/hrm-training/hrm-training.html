Human Resource Management -Training Course

This comprehensive course offers an in-depth exploration of the dynamic field of human recruitment and talent acquisition, equipping participants with the skills and strategies needed to attract, assess, and hire top talent in today's competitive job market. Through a blend of theoretical insights and practical exercises, participants will gain a thorough understanding of the recruitment process and its impact on organizational success.

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Audience: Human Resources Professionals, Recruitment Managers, Talent Acquisition Specialists, Hiring Managers, HR Consultants
  • Prerequisites: Basic Computer Skills, Familiarity with HR Concepts, Understanding of Organizational Dynamics, Communication and Interpersonal Skills, Awareness of Recruitment Processes
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • Shortage / Excess, Recruitment Requisition, Interview Invitation, Employment Application, CV Collection Against Requisition, Processing Steps, Evaluate & Review Applicant, Shortlisted Applicant, Interview, Interview Summary List, Offer Letter, Document Collection, Training Plan, Actual Training, Course Evaluation
