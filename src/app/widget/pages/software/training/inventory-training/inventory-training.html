Inventory Management -Training Course

This course provides a comprehensive understanding of inventory management systems and their critical role in maintaining efficient supply chains and operations. In today's dynamic business environment, mastering inventory management is crucial to minimize costs, optimize stock levels, and meet customer demands.

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Audience: Supply Chain Professionals, Inventory Managers, Operations Managers, Procurement Specialists, Retail Managers
  • Prerequisites: Basic Computer Skills, Understanding of Supply Chain Concepts, Familiarity with Business Operations, Basic Knowledge of Inventory Principles, Awareness of Data Analysis Concepts
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • LOI Request ( letter of intent ), Product Master, Material Requisition, Stock Inventory Check, Material Inspection, Store Receipt Voucher, Invoice Collection Log, Internal Request, Stock Issue Voucher, Stock Transfer Request, Stock Transfer, Purchase Return Invoice, Item Query, Stock Count Sheet, Stock Adjustment, Budget Settings
