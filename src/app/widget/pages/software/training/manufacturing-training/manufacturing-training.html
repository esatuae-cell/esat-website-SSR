Manufacturing Management -Training Course

The Manufacturing Management System Implementation course is designed to provide participants with a comprehensive understanding of how to successfully plan, implement, and optimize manufacturing management systems within various production environments. This course equips learners with the knowledge and skills necessary to leverage technology and best practices to enhance operational efficiency, quality control, and overall production effectiveness.

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Audience: Manufacturing Managers, Operations Managers, Industrial Engineers, IT Professionals, Production Supervisors
  • Prerequisites: Basic Understanding of Manufacturing Concepts, Familiarity with Business Operations, Proficiency in Computer Skills, Knowledge of Production Processes, Awareness of Information Technology Principles
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • BOM, Routing, Production Plan, MRP, Job Order, Receive from Production, Production Wastage, Quality Control Request, Quality Test, Routing Master, QC Rules
