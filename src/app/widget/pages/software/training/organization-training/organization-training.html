Organization Development -Training Course

Organization Development " is a course focused on enhancing and optimizing the performance of organizations. It covers strategies, methodologies, and tools to drive positive change, improve efficiency, and foster a healthy organizational culture. Participants learn to analyze and address challenges, implement change initiatives, and cultivate an environment conducive to continuous improvement and growth.

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Audience: Business Leaders and Executives, Human Resources Professionals, Organizational Development Practitioners, Managers and Team Leaders, Change Management Specialists
  • Prerequisites: Basic Understanding of Organizational Structure, Familiarity with Human Resources Concepts, Experience in a Leadership or Management Role, Knowledge of Workplace Dynamics, Awareness of Organizational Challenges and Opportunities
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day
