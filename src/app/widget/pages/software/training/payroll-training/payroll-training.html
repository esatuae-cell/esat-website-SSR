Payroll Management -Training Course

The Payroll Management Essentials course is designed to provide participants with a comprehensive understanding of the fundamental principles and practices of payroll management. This course equips participants with the knowledge and skills necessary to effectively handle payroll processes, ensuring accurate and timely compensation for employees while complying with legal and regulatory requirements.

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Audience: Human Resources Professionals, Payroll Administrators, Accounting Professionals, Small Business Owners, Administrative Assistants or Office Managers
  • Prerequisites: Basic Accounting Knowledge, Familiarity with Payroll Terminology, Understanding of Labor Laws and Regulations, Proficiency in Spreadsheet Software (e.g., Microsoft Excel), Knowledge of Employee Compensation Structures
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • Loan Details, Addition / Deduction, Bulk Addition / Deduction, Daily Time Sheet, Weekly Time Sheet Import, Attendance Details, Monthly Time Sheet, Monthly Attendance Summary, Overtime Priod Approval, Holiday Updation, Payroll Process, Paid Unpaid, SIF Form, Payroll Project Wise Summary / Details, Leave Settlement, Duty Resume, Final Settlement, Change Status/Flight Booking
