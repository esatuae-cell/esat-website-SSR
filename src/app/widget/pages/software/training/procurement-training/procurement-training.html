Procurement Management -Training Course

The Procurement Management Strategies course offers a comprehensive exploration of the principles, techniques, and best practices essential for effective procurement within modern organizations. This course provides participants with the knowledge and skills required to navigate the complexities of procurement processes, vendor relationships, and supply chain optimization to achieve cost-efficient and sustainable outcomes.

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Audience: Procurement Professionals, Supply Chain Managers, Purchasing Officers, Project Managers, Business Operations Managers
  • Prerequisites: Basic Business Knowledge, Understanding of Supply Chain Concepts, Familiarity with Negotiation Principles, Proficiency in Communication Skills, Knowledge of Financial Analysis
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • Business Partners, Purchase Requisition, Service Purchase Request, Purchase Enquiry, Quotation From Supplier, Comparison Sheet, Purchase Order, Delivery Schedule, Price History, Evaluation Target Types, Evaluation Settings, Supplier Evaluation
