Real Estate Management - Training Course

The Property and Lease Management Mastery course offers a comprehensive exploration of the principles, strategies, and practices required to effectively manage both residential and commercial properties, along with the associated lease agreements. This course equips participants with the knowledge and skills necessary to maximize property value, ensure tenant satisfaction, and navigate complex lease agreements.

Course Details

  • Duration: 2 Days
  • Audience: Property Managers, Landlords, Real Estate Professionals, Leasing Agents, Property Owners,
  • Prerequisites: Basic Understanding of Real Estate Concepts, Familiarity with Property Laws and Regulations, Proficiency in Communication Skills, Knowledge of Financial Management Principles, Awareness of Tenant-Landlord Relationships
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • Property Owner, Main Property, Property Master, Property Unit, Property Tree, Property Inquiry / CAF, Reservation / Offer Letter, Property Contract, Amendment, Assignment, Contract Renewal Followup, Resident Master, Consumption Reading, Monthly Turn Over, Rental Billing, Penalty Operation, Charges Invocie, Client Final Settlement, Work Order Subjects, Check List, Unit Inspection, Preventive Maintenance Schedule, Safety and Security Inspection, General Repair, Work Order, Operation Charges
