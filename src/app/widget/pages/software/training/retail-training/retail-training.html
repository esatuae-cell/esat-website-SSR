Retail & POS Management -Training Course

The "Point of Sale (POS) " course provides a concise overview of principles and practices related to the efficient and effective use of POS technology. Participants learn how to leverage POS systems for tasks such as transaction processing, inventory management, and customer relationship management, enhancing their ability to manage retail operations seamlessly.

Course Details

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Audience: Retail Managers, Sales and Customer Service Professionals, Small Business Owners, E-commerce Entrepreneurs, Hospitality Industry Staff
  • Prerequisites: Basic Understanding of Retail Operations, Familiarity with Sales Processes, Knowledge of Customer Service Practices, Basic Computer Literacy, Foundation in Business Concepts
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • POS Configuration, Table Configuration, Loyalty Program, Special Pricing, Cash Drawer Master, Assign Cash Drawer, Daily Sales Collection, Gift Voucher Creation
