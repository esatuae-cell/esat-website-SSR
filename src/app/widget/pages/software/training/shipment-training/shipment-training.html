Shipping/Logistics Management -Training Course

The course on ""Logistics / Shipping Management"" introduces students to a systematic approach to managing and optimizing shipping operations. Participants will learn to leverage dedicated systems for efficient order processing, logistics coordination, and real-time tracking, acquiring crucial skills to improve the effectiveness of shipping processes in a business setting.

Course Details

  • Duration: 2 Days
  • Audience: Logistics and Supply Chain Professionals, Supply Chain Managers, E-commerce Entrepreneurs and Managers, IT and System Implementation Specialists, Business Students and Graduates
  • Prerequisites: Basic Understanding of Logistics, Familiarity with Supply Chain Operations, Knowledge of E-commerce Processes, Basic IT and System Awareness, Foundation in Business Concepts
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • Business Partners, Service Master, Pricing Master, Inquiry, Quotation, Booking, CFS Entry, Shipping Job, Shipment Tracking, Transportation, Shipping Invoice, Consignee Master, Port Master, Item Master
