Warehouse Management -Training Course

The "Warehouse Management" course provides a concise exploration of principles and practices related to efficiently managing and optimizing warehouse operations. Participants learn to leverage specialized software solutions for tasks such as inventory control, order fulfillment, and space utilization, enhancing their ability to streamline and improve warehouse efficiency

Course Details

  • Duration: 2 Days
  • Audience: Warehouse Managers, Logistics and Distribution Professionals, Inventory Control Specialists, Supply Chain Managers, Operations and Warehouse Staff
  • Prerequisites: Basic Understanding of Warehouse Operations, Familiarity with Supply Chain Concepts, Knowledge of Inventory Management, Basic Computer Literacy, Foundation in Business Operations
  • Course Fee: AED 2500 / Participant Per Day

Topics

  • Item Master, Warehouse Master, Pallet Master, Warehouse Inquiry, Warehouse Quote, Warehouse Agreement, Advance Shipping Note, Stock Receipt ( inbound ) GRN, Stock Transfer Request, Dispatch Request, Pick List, Stock Transfer, Stock Issue / Dispatch, Invoice, Stock Return, Business Partners, Consignee Master, Physical Inventory, Stock Adjustment, Racking Types
